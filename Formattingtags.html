Formatting -- Formatting in html , refers to se of html elements 
for style and structure of the text content. 
HTML provides wide range of formatting tags for  structuring & styling . 

There are two types of formmating -- 
1.Visual formatting -- used to directly affect the apperance of the text content . 
Often they do not carry the semantic meaning of the beyond the visual style that apply. 

These tags are common in previous versions of html , their use discouraged in favour od css.

For ex -- Bold tag , Italic tag , Underline tag , strikethrough text tag , big tag , small tag , etc.

2.Logical Formatting -- are used to define the meaning or structure of the content rather than 
its apperance . Basically , these tags are semantic tags , means they are providing the information 
about the text content , Which can be useful for accessbility ,seo and readability . 

For ex -- strong tag , Empahsis tag ,mark tag , var tag , abbreviation tag , etc.
